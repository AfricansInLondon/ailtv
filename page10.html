AILTV in the community

 Ailtv in the community

Each one of us belong to a community of some kind. Our committiment is to document activites which goes on in African and African Caribbean Communities.

 What we do for the community

We record and edit  events and present them to public as a way of creating awereness of our progress and challenges. We also record and stream live general entertainment events including Live concerts, fashion and beauty, out door events, community gatherings, African markets not forgeting weddings.

 Why Ailtv

It is  important to realise that information is key and the whole world is driven by information and social media.
We make programs that you will not see on main stream media or TV. 
Technology has made it simple and easier for us to create and tell our own stories anytime anywhere using our own resources.
