Africans In London TV

WELCOME KARIBU
AILTV stands for AFRICANS IN LONDON TV, It started as a simple idea to explore the progress of people of African origin who live and work in London. The aim and objective of AILTV is to tell our own story/stories by making high quality programs and documenteries about people of African origin.

AILTV is providing a platform to producers and writers to create and produce programs without limitations.
Moreover people of African origin has made a tremendous progress, and have intergrated and not only are part of British culture, but also many Africans and people the caribbean acuppy positions in education, health, transport, media, business and entertainment.
